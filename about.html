Timeline

Our Story

  • 1987-1996

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Ricardo Velasquez started DJing at the age of 13 in the South Bay. Back then, it was strictly vinyl records. It wasn't easy finding records in his neighborhood.

  • 1996-2008

    WP Records

    At the age of 21, Ricardo established his own personal DJ shop in the city of Lomita. This marked the birth of WP Records, a hub catering to all DJ needs, which operated until 2008.

  • 2008

    Event Planning

    As time changed, so did the business. It eventually transitioned into an events company. Knowing and working with so many vendors in our industry allowed us to serve our clients better as we could offer them many services under one roof.

  • 2023

    Cre8tiveLA Weddings and Events

    Fast forward to 2023, we've been in the industry for over 25 years, specializing in DJs, MCs, and Wedding Planning. Now known as Cre8tiveLA Weddings and Events, or Cre8tiveLA.com, your go-to for all event-related needs.
