Why Cre8tiveLA?

Cre8tiveLA offers top-notch DJ services, MCs, and event planning with over 25 years of industry experience. We take pride in providing the finest event services thanks to our loyal clientele. Our extensive collection of high-end equipment, including wireless uplights, wedding monograms, and textured lighting, ensures your event is nothing short of exceptional.

All of our vendors are top-notch, and we only work with vendors that meet the highest quality in their industry. Due to the high referral rate from our clientele, we decided it was time to join the online media world, this will give potential clients the opportunity to see the quality of our work.

Cre8tiveLA, based in the City of Lomita, offers free consultations by appointment at our central office. For any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us. Rest assured, with over two decades of experience, our track record speaks for itself.
